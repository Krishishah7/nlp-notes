# Word Frequency Analysis & Visualization

Word frequency analysis counts how often words appear in a text.

## Why Word Frequency Matters
- Identifies important terms
- Helps understand dominant topics
- Useful for summarization and keyword extraction

## Frequency Analysis
Words are counted after preprocessing such as tokenization and stopword removal.

## Visualization
Bar charts are commonly used to visualize the most frequent words.
Visualization helps quickly interpret textual patterns.
